Cowpital Records

Bovine Bliss is rich, sweet, creamy frozen album made from variously flavored cream and milk products churned and stirred to a smooth consistency during the freezing process. You can expect to hear a generous helping of gelatin, eggs, fruits, nuts, and more in each and every song. The definition of Bovine Bliss is a soft, frozen treat made from milk fat.
